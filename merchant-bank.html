Beyond profiles, spaces, and events, Maia is building a merchant banking layer, warm introductions to capital, and a network that stands behind the ventures its members build. The next phase of what membership means. Not yet open, worth being early to.

What the capital layer will be

Not a fund you pitch.
A network that backs you.

Membership already puts you in the room with the people who move capital. The layer now in development turns proximity into outcomes, and crosses a line no private club has: when a member is building, Maia intends not only to make the introduction, but to stand behind the ventures it believes in. Built deliberately. Coming soon.

Two ways capital will move

Maia backs members.
Members back members.

One: The house

Maia backs its own.

No private club in the world invests in its own members. That is the line Maia is built to cross. When the layer opens, the network intends to stand behind select members' ventures, alongside the members building them. A house with skin in the success of the people inside it. This is the part nobody else can copy, because nobody else is built to.

Two: The network

Members back members.

Inside Maia, the Investor pillar (family offices, angels, allocators) and the Entrepreneurship pillar (founders and operators) are already in the same room. When a member is raising, Maia will make the warm introduction to the members who genuinely fit, before it ever reaches the open market. Smart introductions between two people who were always meant to meet, never a cold pitch, never financial advice.

How it will work

Smart introductions.
No financial advice.

01

A warm introduction

When a member is building, Maia introduces the right people inside the network, "let me introduce you to someone who wants to build this with you." The connection is the product.

02

The network is the diligence

Everyone has been nominated and vetted to the same global bar. That shared standard is the first layer of trust, so conversations move warmer and faster here than anywhere outside.

03

The decision is the members'

The conversation, the terms, and the decision belong to the members in the room. Maia makes the introduction and steps back, an intermediary, never an adviser.

Who it is being built for

Builders, backers,
and the companies behind them.

For members building

Founders and operators in the Entrepreneurship pillar reach capital that already understands them, introduced through people they trust, not pitched cold into an inbox.

For members backing

The Investor pillar (family offices, angels, allocators) sees network-vetted ventures from members held to a global bar, before they reach the open market.

For corporate members

Companies, not only individuals, join the same room, whether they are deploying capital or seeking it. One standard of admission applies to all.

Aligned by design

Everyone has cleared the same bar. The network itself is the first layer of diligence, so trust is the starting point, not the thing you have to establish.

Why it compounds

The first ventures
become the proof.

A capital layer earns trust by working once, in the open, inside the network. The first ventures introduced and backed through Maia become the reference for the next. As members raise and build through the network, membership compounds in value for everyone already inside it: an athlete's wellness brand, a musician's label, an operator's second act, each made a little more real by the room around it.

On the horizon

A wider circle,
a smaller ticket.

Further out, Maia is exploring fractional participation in member ventures, a way to open a member's raise to a wider circle of the network than a traditional cap table allows. It is an aspiration, not a feature, and it will be scoped carefully and lawfully before anything is ever offered. We share it because it is part of where this is going, not because it is here today.
