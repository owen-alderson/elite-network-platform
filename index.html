Why Aether

For the talent
moving fields.

The athlete launching the beauty brand. The musician opening the restaurant. The model raising for a fashion line. Aether is built for proven talent turning reputation in one field into a venture in another — and the introductions that make those crossings happen.

01

Talents in motion

Members are building their next venture in an industry adjacent to the one that made them. Aether is the room where that crossing happens — for the people who've already done the first thing well.

02

Anchored in real rooms

Curated access to private clubs and members' houses — starting with Spring Place in New York and Los Angeles, with London, Milan, and Paris in the pipeline. The crossings happen face to face.

03

Vetted, then introduced

Members are reviewed against the global bar in their original field — and the credibility of what they're building next. Introductions follow when trust and timing make sense.
