The capital layer

A network that
invests in its own.

Beyond profiles, spaces, and events, Maia is building a capital layer — connecting members raising for their next venture with the investors and operators already inside the network. The next phase of what membership means.

What the capital layer is

More than introductions.

01

Warm introductions

When a member is raising — a fund, a brand, a company — Maia makes warm introductions to the investors, family offices, and operators inside the network who genuinely fit. Connections through people you trust, never cold outreach.

02

Maia backs members

Maia is not only a matchmaker. The platform and the network around it back members directly. No private club in the world invests in its own members — that is the line Maia is built to cross.

03

The proof loop

The first ventures backed through Maia become the proof. As members raise and build through the network, membership compounds in value for everyone inside it.

Two sides of one network

Members who build.
Members who back.

For members building

Founders and operators in the Entrepreneurship pillar reach capital that already understands them — introduced through the network, not pitched cold into an inbox.

For members investing

The Investor pillar — family offices, angels, allocators — sees curated, network-vetted ventures from members held to a global bar, before they reach the open market.

Aligned by design

Everyone in the room has been nominated and vetted to the same standard. Conversations move on trust, because the network itself is the first layer of diligence.

Cross-pollination, funded

An Olympic athlete building a wellness brand. A musician launching a label. The capital layer is what turns the adjacent venture every member is already building into something real.

What's live, what's next

The connections first.
The capital follows.

The pilot proves the network — that the right members, in one place, create real introductions and real momentum. The capital layer scales from there, deliberately, as the network deepens. This is a marker of where Maia is going: an invitation to build and back the next venture alongside people held to the same bar as you.
