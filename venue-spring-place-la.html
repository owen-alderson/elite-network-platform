9800 Wilshire Boulevard · Beverly Hills · Los Angeles, CA 90212

About the space

Spring Place Beverly Hills is the West Coast home of the global members' club — 40,000 square feet of design-forward workspace, dining, and event programming, plus a 6,500-square-foot rooftop terrace looking out over the city. Designed by WHY Architecture, it opened in October 2018 as the second Spring Place location after Tribeca.

It is the natural physical home for Aether's Los Angeles community — the same calibre of room as New York, on the same coast as the entertainment, technology, and venture capital communities Aether members operate in.

The Aether connection

◈

Co-founded by Alessandro Cajrati Crivelli

Both Spring Place locations were co-founded by Alessandro Cajrati Crivelli — Aether's co-founder and partnership lead. The Beverly Hills club extends the same philosophy as Tribeca: bring together exceptional people across art, business, technology, and culture, and give them a space that matches their ambition. It is Aether's founding West Coast partner for exactly that reason.

Facilities

◈

Restaurant & Bar

Full-service restaurant and lounge. Private catering available for Aether events.

◈

Rooftop Terrace

6,500 sq ft rooftop with views over Beverly Hills. Available for private events and member bookings.

◈

Private Screening Room

Dedicated screening room for premieres, member events, and private bookings — central to the LA programming calendar.

◈

Private Offices

Dedicated office suites for members who run their day from the club. Concierge support included.

◈

Private Dining Rooms

Reservable private rooms for board meetings, family gatherings, and small Aether dinners.

◈

Open Co-working

Hot desks and open lounges for members who prefer the open floor — quiet enough to take a call, social enough to meet a peer.

◈

Phone Booths

Soundproof booths for private calls between meetings — across the floor and on the rooftop.

◈

Concierge

World-class concierge service available to all Aether members during their visit.
