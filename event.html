About this event

An intimate evening for Maia members in New York — hosted at Spring Place's private dining room in Tribeca. The format is unstructured. No panels, no speakers, no agenda. A room of exceptional people with time to talk.

Past evenings have resulted in co-investments, research collaborations, introductions across continents, and at least two ongoing partnerships that began over dinner. None of that was planned.
