Why partner with Aether

What your space gains.

Association

Aether is invite-only and globally elite. Partnership signals that your space meets that standard — a meaningful distinction in a market full of members clubs.

Revenue

Members access partner spaces for events, dinners, and meetings. You set the terms. Aether handles logistics, member communication, and curation.

Network effect

Your venue becomes a node in a global network. Members in Milan know Spring Place in New York. Members arriving in London will know your space before they land.

Selectivity guaranteed

Aether's vetting doesn't stop at membership. We coordinate with partners on event formats to ensure every gathering serves the quality of your space — not just our network.

First-mover in your city

We launch city by city. The first partner in each city is the anchor — the default home for Aether members passing through. That positioning doesn't become available twice.

Zero marketing overhead

We bring the members. You provide the space and experience. The Aether platform handles discovery, booking, and communication end-to-end.
